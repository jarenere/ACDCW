The power supply subassembly converts the 110 or 230 volt mains current to three DC voltages: 3.3V, 5V and 12V.

In case you need to replace the PSU or connect custom hardware requiring power (such as extra fans), here is the pinout of the connector:

PinVoltage
1 +3.3V
2 +5V
3 GND
4 GND
5 GND
6 +12V
